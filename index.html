Technical Specifications

Complete technical details

Component Specification
LoRa Module LoRaWAN 1.0.3, 915 MHz (US), 14 dBm TX power
GNSS Receiver Multi-constellation (GPS, GLONASS, Galileo), ±2.5m accuracy
Processor ARM Cortex-M4, 168 MHz, 512 KB Flash, 128 KB SRAM
Power Supply 12-36V DC input, <5 µA standby, battery backup supported
Connectivity LoRaWAN, optional cellular (LTE-M/NB-IoT)
Sensors Temperature, Humidity, Accelerometer, Gyroscope (optional: Pressure, Light)
Enclosure IP67 rated, operating temperature -40°C to +85°C
Dimensions 120mm × 80mm × 35mm
Weight 180g
